Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our MMO game service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen username for game identification
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored using SHA-256 hashing

    • 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

    When you use our Service, we may automatically collect:

    • IP address and connection information
    • Device information and operating system
    • Game activity and gameplay statistics
    • Session duration and frequency
    • Character data (name, level, position, health, inventory)

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use the collected information for the following purposes:

    • Account Management: To create and manage your game account
    • Game Services: To provide, maintain, and improve the game experience
    • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and unauthorized access
    • Communication: To send important service updates and notifications
    • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve game features
    • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

    3. Data Security

    Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

    Our security practices include:

    • Password Hashing: All passwords are hashed using SHA-256 encryption before storage
    • Prepared Statements: Database queries use parameterized statements to prevent SQL injection attacks
    • Secure Sessions: Session tokens are randomly generated and securely managed
    • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent XSS attacks
    • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates are applied regularly

    4. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting our support team.

    We will retain and use your information as necessary to:

    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Resolve disputes
    • Enforce our agreements
    • Prevent fraud and abuse

    5. Information Sharing

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

    • Legal Requirements: When required by law or in response to valid legal processes
    • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
    • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
    • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

    6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your game experience. These technologies help us:

    • Maintain your session and keep you logged in
    • Remember your preferences and settings
    • Analyze usage patterns and improve performance
    • Prevent fraudulent activity

    7. Children's Privacy

    Age Restriction: Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13.

    We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

    8. Your Privacy Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • Access: Request access to your personal information
    • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
