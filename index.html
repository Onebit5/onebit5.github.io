OneTek Engine 2

Built with modern C++20 practices, the engine supports PBR rendering, post-processing effects, and includes development tools with Dear ImGui integration.

Technologies: C++20, Vulkan, DirectX 12, PhysX, SDL2, Assimp, ImGui, CMake
